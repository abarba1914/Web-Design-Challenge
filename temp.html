Max Temperature


This is the temperature as it relates to latitude. 0 degrees is the location of the equator and we can see that the temperatures tend to be high as you get closer to the equator.

This graph is also representing that the temperatures in the southern hemisphere are higher than that of the northern hemisphere. Possibly to be expected during that time of year (January).
